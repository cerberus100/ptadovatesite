Maria's Journey: From Limited Options to Full Recovery

"I felt trapped with no options. Helping Hands opened doors I didn't know existed - they gave me choices that changed everything."

Maria came to us with a diabetic foot ulcer, facing only one devastating option: amputation. Our advocates found her multiple treatment choices, secured $18,000 in financial assistance, and connected her with specialists who saved her leg.

Outcome: Complete healing, leg saved, returned to work - all because she had choices
