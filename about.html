About Helping Hands

Dedicated patient advocates committed to ensuring every person receives the healthcare they need, regardless of their financial situation.

Our Mission

To eliminate barriers between patients and quality wound care by providing personalized advocacy, financial assistance navigation, and comprehensive support throughout the healing journey.

Our Promise

No patient should suffer from lack of access to proper wound care. We bridge the gap between medical need and available resources.
