Our Mission

True North Advocates is a non-profit organization committed to connecting patients in need with healthcare providers who prioritize patient-centered care, compassionate treatment, and accessible wound care services.

💙 Patient-First Providers

We partner with healthcare professionals who demonstrate exceptional patient care, offer sliding scale fees, accept various insurance plans, and are committed to treating all patients with dignity and respect.

Network Benefits

Join a network of like-minded providers, receive patient referrals, access continuing education resources, and make a meaningful impact in your community's healthcare landscape.
